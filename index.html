Happenings:

Whether you’re on foot or utilizing a wheelchair, finding an easy “walking” route can be a challenge due to Austin’s incomplete sidewalk network, poorly maintained sidewalks, debris on sidewalks, or areas of high crime.

Help:

Hey, I’m Walking Here! is a web app that maps walking routes, showing you places along each route where other users have reported sidewalk issues, where pedestrian deaths have occurred, or where crime rates are high.

Future Happenings:

Users can save their routes and add markers to alert others of sidewalk issues. User data will automatically become available to anyone locally using the app.
